Nguyen Phan

Nguyen Phan obtained his B.Sc. (Honors) Degrees in Computer Science from University of Information Technology (UIT), Vietnam National University Ho Chi Minh City (VNUHCM) in 2021. He currently works as a Data Scientist at Zalo. Prior to that, he Work as AI Engineer, Apllied Scientist at Emage Development JSC and Vinbrain JSC respectively. His working and research interests include Computer Vision and Applications, Deep Learning, and Data Science.
